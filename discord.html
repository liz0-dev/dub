SERVER GUIDELINES

Discord Rules

To maintain a positive and productive environment, we have some basic rules that all members must follow:

01

Respect Other Members

Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or bullying will not be tolerated.

02

No Mic Spamming

Keep voice channels clear for effective communication, especially during raids.

03

Use Appropriate Channels

Post messages in the appropriate channels to keep our server organized.

04

No Advertising

Don't advertise other Discord servers, services, or products.

05

Follow Discord TOS

Adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

06

No Clan Secrets

Don't share sensitive clan information with non-members.
