DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Our Building Philosophy

Our base designs follow specific principles developed over hundreds of wipe cycles. These core concepts guide how we approach base building.

Defense in Depth

Multiple layers of protection ensure raiders waste resources before reaching vital areas.

Space Efficiency

Maximize the utility of each square of foundation space for optimal resource usage.

Visibility Control

Strategic visibility that gives defenders advantage while limiting raider information.

Adaptability

Designs that can be expanded and modified throughout the wipe as clan needs evolve.
