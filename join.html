CLAN STANDARDS

Membership Requirements

We maintain high standards for our members to ensure our clan remains competitive and cohesive.

Experience

Minimum 1,000 hours in Rust with proficiency in core game mechanics like building, raiding, and PvP.

Communication

Must have a working microphone and be comfortable with voice communication in English.

Activity

Able to play at least 20 hours per week and participate in scheduled clan activities.

Teamwork

Willingness to contribute to clan operations and follow leadership decisions during raids.

Maturity

Minimum age of 18 years and ability to handle conflict maturely without toxicity.

Recording

Ability to record gameplay footage for clan content and raid documentation.

HOW TO JOIN

Application Process

Becoming a RustWraith member involves a thorough vetting process to ensure you're a good fit for our community.

1

Submit Application

Fill out our detailed application form with your Rust experience, skills, and availability.

2

Discord Interview

If your application is promising, you'll be invited for a voice chat with our recruitment team.

3

Probation Period

Successful applicants join as recruits for a 2-week probation to assess compatibility.

4

Full Membership

Recruits who demonstrate skill, teamwork, and dedication are promoted to full members.
